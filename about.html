About Us

Delivering Multifaceted Healthcare Solutions for Wellness

What We are About

At Akansoh Health Service, we are committed to transforming the lives of individuals with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities (IDD) through compassionate, innovative, and personalized healthcare solutions. Our goal is to empower those we serve by providing comprehensive support that enhances their quality of life and fosters independence.

With a team of highly skilled and dedicated professionals passionate about making a difference, we offer a range of services tailored to meet everyone’s unique needs. Our holistic approach combines Homemaker/chore, Companion Service, Shift Nursing Care, and Community Participation support. Transportation Services and life skills training ensures that everyone receives the support they deserve.

Mission Statement

Akansoh Health Service’s mission is to support individuals with developmental disabilities to achieve greater independence and function at their highest potential.
We are dedicated to improving the lives of individuals who are vulnerable and underserved. We strive to provide individuals with the support needed knowing that individuals are better equipped when anchored in a cohesive support system. We believe individuals have a right to choose their own destiny.

Vision Statement

Akansoh Health Service’s vision is to foster a continuum of support services in every community we serve. Also, to be the region’s provider of choice for individuals and families seeking services, and as the region’s employer of choice for qualified and committed professionals.

Why Choose Us

Personalized Care

Expert Team

Community Integration
