Health Services and Nursing Home

Our Comprehensive Services

Companion Services

We provide non-medical assistance, observation, supervision and care for the health of adults with Intellectual Developmental Disability in their private homes and in the community. Our companion service is designed to support individuals as they navigate the road to living independently and alleviate feelings of loneliness and isolation.

Homemaker/Chore

Support for individuals with intellectual disability to perform personal care, medication management, housework, dressing, grooming, and meal preparation. We help unlock wellness and independence while maintaining open communication with family and caregivers.

Transportation Services

We provide professional and convenient transportation services to and from your home for medical appointments, community participation, and essential outings. Our services ensure safety and reliability for all clients.

Community Participation Support

We support individuals with intellectual and developmental disability to integrate with the community, participate in meaningful activities, and develop social networks. Our focus is on inclusion and empowerment.

Shift Nursing

Our nurses are experienced and provide a high level of care with compassion for every family. We deliver nursing care solutions with a high level of professionalism, including medication management, personal care, monitoring, and safety assurance.

Wellness Consultation

Our expert team provides personalized wellness consultations to help you achieve optimal health. We assess your needs and guide you with tailored recommendations for nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle improvements.
